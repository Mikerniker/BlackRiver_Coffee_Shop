Recipes

Caffe Americano

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces (2 shots) BlackRiver espresso

  • 4 ounces hot water

Instructions

Make the espresso using an espresso machine, manual espresso maker, French press, or Moka Pot. Heat water using your espresso machine or in a tea pot to about 190 degrees Fahrenheit. Pour it into the espresso. Enjoy!
